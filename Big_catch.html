Monthly Fishing Competition

Big Catch

The fishing competition for August is all about the biggest fish of any species.

To participate in this event members must send nominations through to info@cardwellsportfishingclub.com or text to 0432 758483 to enable registration for the event.

Please also indicate whether you will be attending the gold coin BBQ so we can handle the catering by phoning the secretary on 0432 758483.

Non-members will need to pay $10, download and fill in a registration form together with the rules (see below) and then send the completed form and registration fee to info@cardwellsportfishingclub.com
