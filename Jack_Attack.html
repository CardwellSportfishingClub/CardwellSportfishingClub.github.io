Monthly Fishing Competition

Grunter Hunter

The fishing competition for June is all about the Javelin. It is a combination event with the fishing competition commencing on Friday, followed by the State of Origin on the Sunday Night.

To participate in this event members must send nominations through to cardwellsportfishingclub@hotmail.com or text to 0432 758483 to enable registration for the event.

Please also indicate whether you will be attending the State of Origin and Pasta night so we can handle the catering by phoning the secretary on 0432 758483.

Non-members will need to pay $10, download and fill in a registration form together with the rules (see below) and then send the completed form and registration fee to cardwellsportfishingclub@hotmail.com
