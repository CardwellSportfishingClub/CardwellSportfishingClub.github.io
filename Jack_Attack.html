Monthly Fishing Competition

Jack Attack

The fishing competition for April is all about the feisty mangrove jack. Mark Ward for Fishing & Boating offered this advice for getting on to one of these nice fish. “……early morning, with still water, using live baits and lures and of course they like structures, mangroves etc”. Dan Kaggelis also in last months Fishing & Boating says jacks are being caught in big numbers on skipping frogs and minnow lures under mangroves deep in the shade. To participate in this event members must send nominations through to cardwellsportfishingclub@hotmail.com or text to 0432 758483 to enable registration for the event. Please also indicate whether you will be attending the weigh in and BBQ so we can handle the catering. Rules will be sent via email but can also be downloaded from the website as well as the registration form. Non-members will need to pay $10, download and fill in a registration form together with the rules and then send the completed form and registration fee to cardwellsportfishingclub@hotmail.com
